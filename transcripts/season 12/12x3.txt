Mr. Frensky is putting a trash bag in his garbage truck in front of the Barnes’ house. Binky brings another bag.

Mr. Frensky: Hey, Binky. Did you watch the Lance DVD?

Binky: Seven times. But I bet Lance's mom never told him he had to stop training.

Mr. Frensky: Your mother said that?

Binky: Well, no, but she said I can only bike in the park.

Mr. Frensky: It's true. There aren't enough bike lanes on the streets, but I hope that'll change next Tuesday at the election.

Binky: An election? What's that got to do with bike lanes?

Mr. Frensky: People are gonna vote on whether the town should build more of them.

Binky: That seems like a no-brainer.

Mr. Frensky: You'd think so, but a lot of people don't care about bike lanes.

Binky: They don't?

Mr. Frensky: And some people who say they do care don't vote.

Binky: Well, then I'll just make them care and make everyone vote.

Mr. Frensky: Good luck.
